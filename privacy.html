Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 10, 2025

EchoedWild collects no personal data by default. However, we use several features that may involve third-party services. This policy outlines what those are and how they may affect your privacy.

Third-party services

We use external services such as Google Fonts, embedded music players, and chatbots. These services may collect usage data such as device type, browser, or interaction behavior. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Music Widgets

We embed music using third-party services. These may track play counts, device type, or location. EchoedWild does not store this data.

Chatbot

Our chatbot is powered by a third-party provider. Conversations may be logged or analyzed to improve functionality. EchoedWild does not store or access these logs.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can accept or decline cookies via our popup. Your choice is stored locally and not shared with third parties.

Email Subscription

When you subscribe to our newsletter, your email is securely stored by Buttondown. We do not share or sell your information. You may unsubscribe at any time.

Contact Form

Messages sent through our contact form are used only to respond to inquiries. We do not store or share this data.

Custom UI Elements

Our site includes custom cursors and scrollbars for aesthetic purposes. These do not collect or store any user data.

Analytics

We currently do not use analytics. If added in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect what data is collected and how it is used.

Questions?

If you have questions about privacy or want to request data removal, please contact us via our Contact Us page.
