Sumatran Tiger

Critically Endangered

Estimated remaining: ~400

Habitat

Tropical forests of Sumatra, Indonesia.

Threats

  • Poaching
  • Habitat loss due to agriculture and palm oil
  • Human-wildlife conflict

Conservation efforts

Habitat protection, anti-poaching patrols, breeding programs.
