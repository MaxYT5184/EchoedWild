Chimpanzee

Endangered

Estimated remaining: 172,700–299,700

Habitat

Rainforests, savannas, and woodland mosaics across Central and West Africa.

Threats

  • Deforestation and habitat fragmentation
  • Poaching for bushmeat and pet trade
  • Disease outbreaks (e.g. Ebola)

Conservation efforts

Protected areas, anti-poaching patrols, ecotourism, and rehabilitation centers. WWF and regional programs support habitat preservation and community education.
