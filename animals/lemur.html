Lemur

Critically Endangered

Estimated remaining: 98% of species endangered

Habitat

Rainforests, spiny forests, and dry deciduous forests across Madagascar.

Threats

  • Deforestation and slash-and-burn agriculture
  • Illegal pet trade and hunting
  • Climate change and invasive species

Conservation efforts

Protected areas, reforestation, captive breeding, and community-based conservation programs that support both wildlife and Malagasy people.
